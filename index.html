    Dj Riki Nairobi

    DJ Riki Nairobi (Rikesh Laxmidas Chauhan) is a Club DJ and music producer from Nairobi, Kenya. Playing primarily Bollywood, EDM, Trap, Hip Hop, Ragga, House, Techno, Trance, Pop, Dubstep. DJ Riki Nairobi has over 10 years experience in music producing and being a DJ. He is currently getting his music productions played on commercial radio & internet radio stations in Kenya, India, Pakistan and many other places.
